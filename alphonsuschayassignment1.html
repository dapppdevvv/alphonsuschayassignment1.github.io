JURONG BIRD PARK

Asia's Largest Bird Paradise

About Us

Jurong Bird Park is an aviary and tourist attraction in Jurong, Singapore. The bird park covers an area of 0.2 square kilometres on the western slope of Jurong Hill, the highest point in the Jurong region.

Activities

Here’s something no-birdy should miss!

Come join us at our daily Bird shows where you can interact with our very own tropical friends to see them perform amazing stunts. Why not hop on our trams for an exciting journey around our park or join our tropical friends for their feeding sessions! Shows

KING OF THE SKIES

Showcasing the fiercest and most formidable birds known to mankind, King of the skies brings together birds that are on top of their food chain such as the the white-tailed sea eagle, the hooded vulture and the Harris's hawk. Check out these magnificent predators in action with our Bird trainers!

Show Timings: Daily 10am/4pm
Zone: Hawk Arena

High Flyers Show

Catch our most popular birds in action! From watching our Amazon parrots race and compete against each other to our friendly Macaws and Sun birds known for their nifty talents. These talented birds are ones to watch. Catch the Grand Finale where you will witness one of the world's biggest flock of birds consisting of mesmerising colours swoop in to end the show with a blast!

Show Timings: Thursdays to Sundays, 11am/3pm
Zone: Pools Amphitheatre

Getting There

Making your way to Jurong Bird Park is easy. No matter where you’re starting your journey from, there are several quick and convenient ways to get here.

Address:
Address: 2 Jurong Hill, Singapore 628925

Opening Hours:
Jurong Bird Park is open on Thursdays to Sundays, eve of Public Holidays and Public Holidays. The park will also remain open during selected school holidays. Operating hours from 8:30am to 6pm.

Getting there by Public Transport?:
Take the East-West MRT line bound for Tuas Link, and alight at Boon Lay (EW27). From the Boon Lay bus interchange, take Bus No. 194, which will take you straight to Jurong Bird Park.

Getting there by Self Drive/Taxi:
Via Pan Island Expressway (PIE) from the City

1)Take the PIE towards Jurong/Tuas

2)Take Exit 36, and turn left onto Jalan Bahar towards Jalan Boon Lay

3)Turn right at Jalan Ahmad Ibrahim to reach Jurong Bird Park
